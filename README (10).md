## Starting the Conversation

### Understanding prompt engineering

Prompt engineering is about writing prompts that guide the AI to give you the most useful and precise answers.

The AI tool takes in what the user typed in a request, or the button they clicked. This helps them figure out what they want and what context is needed.

The AI tool then passes your request through a specific prompt template written by a prompt engineer. The prompt tells the tool how to respond based on what the user needs.

The AI tool gives a response based on the prompt. The tool adjusts it (like fixing the format or tone) to make sure it meets the user’s expectations and is easy to understand.

In this step, I will be experimenting with

### Prompt chaining technique

Prompt chaining uses the output of one prompt as input for the next. This lets the AI model build on previous responses. This often generates a more detailed final response.

For example, I asked scenarios where first-time fanfiction authors get stuck and after the answer i asked advice for these scenarios.

### Self-critiquing technique

Another technique is self-critiquing, which means
AI models are great at identifying flaws or areas for improvement in their own responses.
ChatGPT responded to this with ideas to improve the guide/ advice it gave.

A meta-prompt is promt about writing a proper prompt.

### Evaluating the generated prompt

The generated prompt automatically incorporated the question into the prompt and gave a detailed blueprint of what i actually required.

### Refining the generated prompt

I noticed the generated prompt missed instructions I defined, like the role part and minimal words but 
The model’s processing capacity is limited, so it can only focus on the most relevant parts of your prompt.
If the instructions are too complex or lengthy, the model might skip or simplify parts to stay within token limits (there is a limit to how many words your AI model can take from you and then generate out).

To get the best results, usually:
Include just 1-2 techniques in your initial prompt to keep it focused.
Once the AI responds, give feedback and add another 1-2 techniques. This step-by-step approach helps the AI address each part more effectively.
